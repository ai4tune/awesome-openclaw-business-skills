# Skill: Meeting Minutes

## Goal

Convert messy meeting content into an execution-friendly summary.

## Instructions

1. Read the meeting content and infer the main purpose of the discussion.
2. Extract explicit decisions only when supported by the input.
3. Turn follow-up work into action items with owner and deadline when available.
4. Separate unresolved questions from confirmed decisions.
5. Highlight risks, blockers, or ambiguities that may slow execution.

## Output Template

```md
## Meeting Objective

## Key Decisions
- ...

## Action Items
| Action | Owner | Deadline | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| ... | ... | ... | ... |

## Open Questions
- ...

## Risks And Blockers
- ...
```
