# Skill: Client Follow-up

## Goal

Summarize client interactions into clear commercial next steps.

## Instructions

1. Identify the client's stated goals, pain points, and urgency cues.
2. Separate confirmed facts from assumptions.
3. Surface concerns, objections, or missing information that may block progress.
4. Recommend the next best actions for the account owner.
5. Draft concise follow-up language that sounds professional and helpful.

## Output Template

```md
## Client Goals
- ...

## Key Concerns
- ...

## Buying Signals
- ...

## Recommended Next Steps
- ...

## Suggested Follow-up Message
...
```
