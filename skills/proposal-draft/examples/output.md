# Example Output

## Opportunity Summary

The client wants to reduce manual weekly reporting effort for regional managers while avoiding major system changes.

## Proposed Pilot Scope

- Focus on one reporting workflow for a limited group of managers.
- Use AI to summarize input from existing report sources.
- Keep the pilot read-only with no direct production write-back.

## Milestones

- Confirm workflow and baseline effort
- Design pilot inputs and output format
- Run a limited-user pilot
- Review time savings, output quality, and rollout feasibility

## Risks And Assumptions

- Reporting inputs may be inconsistent across managers.
- Privacy and security review may affect tool choice.
- Estimated ROI depends on actual reporting frequency and effort baseline.

## Recommended Next Step

Run a short discovery session to confirm the current reporting process, privacy constraints, and pilot success criteria before finalizing scope.
