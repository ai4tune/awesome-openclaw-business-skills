# Example Output

## Key Concepts

- AI can accelerate drafting, but review and approval remain human responsibilities.
- Confidential customer data should not be shared with public AI tools.
- A safe workflow is draft first, review second, approve third.

## FAQ

- Q: Can AI-generated text be used directly in external materials?
  A: Not without human review and the relevant approval process.
- Q: What is the main risk mentioned in the session?
  A: Over-trusting output and bypassing required review steps.

## Action Checklist

- Use AI for first drafts, not final release.
- Avoid pasting confidential customer information into public tools.
- Route external-facing content through the normal review chain.
