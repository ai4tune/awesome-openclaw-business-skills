# Skill: Training Summary

## Goal

Create a reusable learning asset from training material.

## Instructions

1. Extract the most important concepts and practical takeaways.
2. Reframe dense content into simple language suitable for busy teams.
3. Build a short FAQ from the most likely points of confusion.
4. End with clear action recommendations.
5. Avoid claiming policies or procedures that were not mentioned.

## Output Template

```md
## Key Concepts
- ...

## FAQ
- Q: ...
  A: ...

## Action Checklist
- ...

## Follow-up Resources
- ...
```
