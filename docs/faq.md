# FAQ

## Why only five skills first?

Because a focused first release is easier to understand, maintain, and improve than a large unfocused list.

## Are these production-ready?

They are baseline samples. They are intended to demonstrate practical structure, quality expectations, and adaptation paths.

## Can I use these in a company?

Yes, but review internal privacy, compliance, and workflow requirements before deployment.

## Why are some advanced workflows not public?

Because deeply integrated workflows usually depend on industry logic, private knowledge, or customer-specific process design.
