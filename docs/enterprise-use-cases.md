# Enterprise Use Cases

## Sales Follow-up

Turn call notes, WeChat summaries, or CRM comments into a structured view of client needs, objections, and next-step actions.

Recommended skill: `client-followup`

## Internal Meetings

Convert transcript-heavy meetings into decisions, owners, deadlines, and risk items without forcing everyone to read a long transcript.

Recommended skill: `meeting-minutes`

## Management Reporting

Combine fragmented weekly notes into a concise report a manager can skim quickly.

Recommended skill: `weekly-report`
