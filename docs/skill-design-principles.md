# Skill Design Principles

## 1. Start With A Real Workflow

Every skill should solve a recognizable business task, not a vague AI idea.

## 2. Optimize For Fast Trust

A reader should understand the business value in under a minute.

## 3. Structure Beats Brilliance

Good business skills usually win by producing consistent outputs, not by sounding clever.

## 4. Show Input And Output

Examples are mandatory because they reduce ambiguity and improve reuse.

## 5. Baseline Public, Deeply Custom Later

Public skills should stay generic enough to reuse. Industry-specific and enterprise-connected variants can be layered on top later.

## 6. Explain Limitations

Good repository content sets expectations clearly and avoids over-claiming.
