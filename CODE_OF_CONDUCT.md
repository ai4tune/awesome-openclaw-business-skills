# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project aims to be a respectful, practical, and professional place to collaborate on business-focused AI skills.

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment:

- Giving clear, constructive feedback
- Sharing real use cases without exposing private information
- Respecting different levels of AI and workflow maturity
- Keeping discussion focused on practical improvement

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment or personal attacks
- Sharing confidential customer information
- Spam, self-promotion without context, or low-effort dumping
- Disruptive or bad-faith behavior

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation for behavior that undermines the health of the project.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in repository discussions, issues, pull requests, and other project-managed spaces.
